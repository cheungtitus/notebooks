# Bitwise vs Logical Operators
## Reference
https://wireshark.askapache.com/docs/man-pages/wireshark-filter.html

Both display and capture filters use the same approach

|Operator| Alternative  | Meaning     |
|--|--------------|-------------|
|&|              | Bitwise And |
|\||              | Bitwise OR  |
|&&| and          | Logical And |
|\|\|| or| Logical OR  |
|!|not|Logical NOT|
